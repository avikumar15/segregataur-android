🤔<b>What does it do</b>
This app segregates memes and useless photos from family photographs.

<b>Completely Offline</b>
All processing takes place on your phone. No Internet required.
Privacy Guaranteed 🤜🤛

⌚<b>Save time</b>
This app uses Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence techniques to sort and segregate your photos and videos. This can drastically reduce the cognitive load 🙇 compared to segregating manually.🧐

✨<b>Save Space</b>
The segregated photos and videos can be deleted with the press of a single button
